About

2023/03/14

Who are you?

My name is Sam Scheele! I’m a master’s student in computer science, specializing in robotics and computational perception.

What is this place?

This is a place for me to write about technical stuff. My current plan is for said stuff to be pretty much entirely math, computer science, and engineering concepts, but I also like to get my hands dirty doing woodworking and electronics projects. Depending on my motivation to write those up and how well I think they’ll translate to a blog format (currently, it seems to me that videos are better for physical things), the blog might end up evolving to cover projects of all sorts.
